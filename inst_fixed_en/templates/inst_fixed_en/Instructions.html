Groups, Appearances and Labels

In Part {{ Constants.part_fixed }} you will interact with {{ Constants.others }} other players. At the beginning of the experiment each of you were be assigned to one of two groups: ● or ▲ (internal symbol), and were assigned an appearance: ◯ or △ (external symbol) and a label (1 to {{ names }}). Your group and appearance are fixed for all {{ Constants.rounds_fixed }} rounds in Part {{ Constants.part_fixed }} while your numeric label will change in each round. There are 3 stages in each round.

Stage 1: Connections

In stage 1, you can propose connections to other players. A connection with another player is active if both you and the other player propose a connection to each other. Each connection you propose will cost you {{ Constants.link_cost }} {{ Constants.exp_currency }}.

Stage 2: Actions

In stage 2, you will choose an action: or . You will earn {{ Constants.exp_currency }} depending on your group, the action you choose, and the number of your active connections choosing the same action as you:

If your group is ● and you:

  • choose , you get {{ Constants.liked_gain }} {{ Constants.exp_currency }} for each active connection choosing (including yourself) and 0 {{ Constants.exp_currency }} for any active connection choosing

  • choose , you get {{ Constants.disliked_gain }} {{ Constants.exp_currency }} for each active connection choosing (including yourself) and 0 {{ Constants.exp_currency }} for any active connection choosing

If your group is ▲ and you:

  • choose , you get {{ Constants.liked_gain }} {{ Constants.exp_currency }} for each active connection choosing (including yourself) and 0 {{ Constants.exp_currency }} for any active connection choosing

  • choose , you get {{ Constants.disliked_gain }} {{ Constants.exp_currency }} for each active connection choosing (including yourself) and 0 {{ Constants.exp_currency }} for any active connection choosing

Stage 3: Results

In stage 3, you will see how many {{ Constants.exp_currency }} you get in that round, which are calculated by the gains of coordinating with your active connections minus the costs of your proposed connections. The border of each player's appearance (◯ or △) shows the action that player chose.

Earnings in the experiment

You will participate for {{ Constants.rounds_fixed }} rounds in Part {{ Constants.part_fixed }}. At the end of the experiment the computer will randomly select one of the {{ Constants.rounds_fixed }} rounds. The number of {{ Constants.exp_currency }} you got in the selected round will be added to the total of points used to determine your payment.
