Instructions Part 1


Groups, Appearances and Labels

In this experiment you will interact with {{ Constants.others }} other players. At the beginning of the experiment each of you will be assigned to one of two groups: ● or ▲ (internal symbol), and will be assigned an appearance: ◯ or △ (external symbol) and a label (1 to 7). Your group and appearance are fixed for all 10 rounds in Part 1 while your numeric label will change in each round. There are 3 stages in each round.

Stage 1: Connections

In stage 1, you can propose connections to other players. A connection with another player is active if both you and the other player propose a connection to each other. Each connection you propose will cost you {{ Constants.link_cost }} {{ Constants.exp_currency }}.

  • choose , you get {{ Constants.disliked_gain }} {{ Constants.exp_currency }} for each active connection choosing (including yourself) and 0 {{ Constants.exp_currency }} for any active connection choosing

If your group is ▲ and you:

  • choose , you get {{ Constants.liked_gain }} {{ Constants.exp_currency }} for each active connection choosing (including yourself) and 0 {{ Constants.exp_currency }} for any active connection choosing

  • choose , you get {{ Constants.disliked_gain }} {{ Constants.exp_currency }} for each active connection choosing (including yourself) and 0 {{ Constants.exp_currency }} for any active connection choosing

Stage 3: Results

In stage 3, you will see how many {{ Constants.exp_currency }} you get in that round, which are calculated by the gains of coordinating with your active connections minus the costs of your proposed connections. The border of each player's appearance (◯ or △) shows the action that player chose.

Earnings in the experiment

You will participate for 10 rounds in Part 1 and for 10 rounds in Part 2. In both parts you will interact with the same other {{ Constants.others }} players. At the end of the experiment the computer will randomly select two rounds, one from Part 1 and one from Part 2. The total number of {{ Constants.exp_currency }} you got in the selected rounds will determine your payment. We will convert the {{ Constants.exp_currency }} into {{ Constants.currency }} using the following exchange rate: {{ Constants.points_exchange }} {{ Constants.exp_currency }} = {{ Constants.currency_exchange }} {{ Constants.currency }}. You will receive your earnings in cash.
